# Ashwake FAQ

## How do I start singleplayer?
- Open **Play** from the main menu.
- Choose **Singleplayer** in Play Hub.
- Create or select your world.

## How do I improve FPS?
- Open **Settings** from the main menu.
- Use the **Performance Preset** in the Performance tab.
- Reduce particles and animation intensity if needed.

## How do I report a bug?
- Open **Guidance** and choose **Bug Report**.
- Include your log file and the steps to reproduce.

## Where are controls and keybinds?
- Open **Guidance** and click **Controls**.
- This opens Minecraft's real Controls screen.
